Why Zero Dependencies?

  • Docker images ~50MBvs 250MB+ with Playwright
  • No supply chain risk— no third-party packages to audit
  • Fast CI— no dependency resolution
  • Portable— works in any Python 3.10+ environment
  • Auditable— entire codebase readable in an afternoon
